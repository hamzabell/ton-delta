## **Part 2: Technical Architecture (The Hybrid-DeFi Model)**

To maximize trust and liquidity, we move the "Long" leg of the trade on-chain.

### **1. The Spot Leg (On-Chain Trust)**

- **Technology:** **STON.fi SDK**.
- **Execution:** When a user deposits USDT, the Smart Contract swaps 50% into the target asset (e.g., DOGE or TON) via STON.fi liquidity pools.
- **Benefit:** The asset sits in a visible, verifiable smart contract. Users can verify solvency on Tonscan.

### **2. The Hedge Leg (Off-Chain Efficiency)**

- **Technology:** **CCXT (Node.js)** connecting to Binance/Bybit.
- **Execution:** The backend opens a **Short Futures** position equal to the Spot holding.
- **Future Upgrade:** Move this to _Storm Trade_ (TON Perps) once liquidity deepens.

### **3. The Transparency Engine (Proof of Reserves)**

- **The Dashboard:** The app displays a read-only API feed showing:
- `Vault Assets (STON.fi): $50,000`
- `Hedge Position (Binance): -$50,000`
- `Delta: 0.00 (Neutral)`

- **Why:** This proves we are not gambling with user funds.
